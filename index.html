The Curriculum

How your body is changing and why breath is the starting point for everything. Diaphragmatic breathing, posture during pregnancy, and the equipment you'll need.

02

First Trimester: Building the Base

Core activation, posture, and mobility work for when your body is adjusting but not yet visibly changed. Pelvic floor basics with a guest expert.

Progressive strength work adapted for your growing belly. Barre-inspired movement with a focus on glutes, hips, and core connection.

I'm in my second or third trimester. Is it too late?

No. Every module meets you where you are. You can start at any point in your pregnancy and get value immediately. Many women start in their third trimester.

Do I need equipment?

Minimal. A yoga mat, a foam roller, and light resistance bands. Liz tells you exactly what you need in Module 1.

How is this different from the Live Prenatal Series?

The Live Series ($599) includes live Zoom sessions with Liz, real-time feedback, expert guest talks, and a private community. The Self-Paced Series ($149) gives you the core workout program and breathwork training on your own schedule, with lifetime access. Many graduates do both.

What if I have a high-risk pregnancy?

Always consult your healthcare provider before starting any exercise program during pregnancy. This program is designed for low-risk pregnancies.

Can I use this for a future pregnancy?

Yes. Lifetime access means you keep it forever. Many women come back for their second and third pregnancies.
